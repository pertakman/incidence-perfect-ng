## What Changed Since RC1

- OTA upload reliability hardening in web UI:
  - pause background polling (`/api/live`, `/api/state`, `/api/network`) while OTA upload is in flight
  - resume normal polling after OTA success/failure
- Purpose:
  - reduce risk of OTA payload integrity mismatch on weaker AP links
  - address observed `OTA failed: sha256 mismatch` during field use
