## What Changed (Highlights)

- OTA updates now require:
  - target version (`YYYY.M.X`) and
  - SHA-256 of the upload payload (integrity gate)
- Version gate rejects stale installs unless `Allow reinstall/downgrade` is enabled.
- Network recovery added:
  - Web: `Recover AP Mode` (forces AP-only and clears STA creds)
  - Physical: hold ACTION during startup (~2 s)
